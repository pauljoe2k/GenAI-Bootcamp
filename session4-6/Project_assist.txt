Name: ClearMail – AI Email Rewriter

Tagline: Transform unclear emails into polished, professional messages in seconds.

Core Features:
1. Instantly rewrites emails based on tone (Formal, Polite, or Friendly).
2. Ensures clarity, correct grammar, and smooth sentence structure.
3. Maintains the original meaning while improving flow and professionalism.
4. Lightweight and fast — processes text within seconds.
5. Simple React-based interface with Node.js backend powered by OpenAI API.
